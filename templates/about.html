Our Story

Born from a simple idea: events should be easy to find, book, and enjoy.

The Beginning

In 2024, our founders were frustrated by the fragmented landscape of event discovery. Great events were happening everywhere, but finding them was painful.

We created EventHive to unify discovery, booking, and experience into one beautiful platform. What began as a weekend project became a global movement.

  • 1M+ events successfully booked
  • 500+ cities worldwide coverage
  • 99.9% platform uptime reliability
